Our Journey

84 Years in the Making

1940

The Beginning

Harold J. Swift opens the first Swift Speed Sender office at the New York harbor, offering hand-carried document delivery to shipping agents.

1952

First Air Route

We launch our first airmail partnership, connecting New York to London in under 48 hours — revolutionary for the era.

1971

International Expansion

Swift Speed Sender opens regional hubs in Frankfurt, Singapore, and São Paulo, covering four continents.

1993

Digital Tracking Launches

We become one of the first couriers to offer customers electronic tracking via fax and then early internet terminals.

2008

100th Country

Our network reaches its 100th country of operation. Over 1 million packages delivered that year alone.

2024

Today

140 countries, 3,000+ team members, real-time GPS tracking, and the same founding promise Harold made in 1940.
